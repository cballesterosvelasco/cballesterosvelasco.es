I can adapt myself when required to work as people work in my job's place.

On my own projects or whenever I can choose. I usually start creating fast spikes without being too picky about them.

So I try to provide fast value and feedback for myself and for the product itself without have to take too many decisions.

Then I iterate the product transforming that spike into a real product. Or discarding that spike when neccesary but not always.

In each iteration I try to improve the product where providing most value, taking into account due dates, milestones and technical debt.

I usually have a pragmatic and realistic point of view.

I love to work with passion, with talent and pushing the limits of what's possible.
